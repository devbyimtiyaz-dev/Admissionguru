About Grant Medical College Admission 2026

Grant Medical College (GMC), Mumbai is one of the oldest and most prestigious government medical colleges in India. Established in 1845, the college has played a significant role in shaping modern medical education in the country. Located in South Mumbai, Grant Medical College is attached to the renowned Sir J. J. Group of Hospitals, one of the largest public hospitals in Asia.

Admission to Grant Medical College for the MBBS course is conducted strictly through the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). Candidates must qualify NEET and participate in the All India Quota (AIQ) or Maharashtra State Quota counseling conducted by the respective authorities. Due to its government status and academic excellence, Grant Medical College has a very high NEET cutoff.

One of the biggest strengths of Grant Medical College is its extensive clinical exposure. The Sir J. J. Group of Hospitals receives a massive daily patient inflow, providing MBBS students with unparalleled hands-on training across multiple medical specialties. This exposure plays a crucial role in developing strong clinical and diagnostic skills.

The college boasts experienced faculty members, advanced laboratories, research facilities, and a well-structured MBBS curriculum. Students are trained rigorously in both theoretical and practical aspects of medicine, making them well-prepared for postgraduate studies and medical practice.

Why Choose Grant Medical College Mumbai?

Grant Medical College is preferred by medical aspirants due to its rich legacy, government status, affordable fee structure, and exceptional clinical exposure. The college has produced some of the finest doctors and medical researchers in India and abroad.

Graduates from Grant Medical College are highly respected and pursue careers in government hospitals, private healthcare, research, academics, and postgraduate medical education. With its long-standing reputation and excellence in healthcare education, Grant Medical College remains a top choice for MBBS admission in India.
