About Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College Admission 2026

Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College is one of the most reputed private medical colleges in India, operating under Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be University). Located in Pune, Maharashtra, the college is known for its strong academic standards, modern infrastructure, and excellent clinical exposure. Every year, a large number of medical aspirants choose Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College for MBBS due to its reputation and consistent academic performance.

Admission to Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College for the MBBS course is conducted strictly through the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). Candidates must qualify NEET and meet the eligibility criteria prescribed by the National Medical Commission (NMC). Counseling and seat allotment are carried out as per the guidelines of the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) for deemed universities.

The college is attached to a well-equipped teaching hospital that provides students with extensive clinical exposure across multiple specialties. With a high patient inflow, modern diagnostic facilities, and experienced faculty, students gain valuable hands-on training throughout their MBBS program. The institution emphasizes a balance between theoretical knowledge and practical medical training.

Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College offers a student-friendly academic environment with advanced laboratories, digital classrooms, libraries, and research facilities. The MBBS curriculum is designed to prepare students for competitive examinations, postgraduate studies, and successful medical practice in India and abroad.
