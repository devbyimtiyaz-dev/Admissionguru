About New Horizon College of Engineering Admission 2026

New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) is a well-established engineering institution in Bangalore, known for its academic excellence, industry-focused education, and professional learning environment.

Admission to B.E / B.Tech programs at NHCE is conducted through KCET for Karnataka state quota seats and COMEDK for management quota seats. Candidates must secure eligible ranks for admission.

NHCE offers undergraduate engineering programs in Computer Science, Information Science, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical Engineering, and related disciplines.

The college provides modern laboratories, innovation centers, industry-integrated learning, and strong placement support to prepare students for competitive engineering careers.
