About Alliance University Admission 2026

Alliance University is a reputed private university in Bangalore, known for its strong academic framework, global exposure, and industry-integrated curriculum. The university offers a vibrant campus environment with a focus on innovation and leadership.

Admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs at Alliance University is conducted through Alliance University Entrance Exam, CUET, and merit-based selection depending on the course.

Alliance University offers programs in Engineering, Management, Law, Commerce, Science, Arts, Liberal Studies, and Computer Applications. The university is particularly well known for its B.Tech, BBA, MBA, Law, and Liberal Arts programs.

The university provides world-class infrastructure, international faculty collaborations, research-driven education, and strong placement support across disciplines.
