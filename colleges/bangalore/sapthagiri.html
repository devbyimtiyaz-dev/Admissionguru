About Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences Admission 2026

Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences & Research Centre (SIMSRC) is a well-known private medical college located in Bangalore, Karnataka. The college is recognized for its modern infrastructure, quality education, and student-friendly academic environment.

Admission to the MBBS course at Sapthagiri Medical College is strictly based on NEET qualification. Eligible candidates must participate in Karnataka state counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA).

The institute is attached to Sapthagiri Hospital, a multi-specialty teaching hospital that provides extensive hands-on clinical exposure with a consistent patient inflow across all major medical departments.

Sapthagiri Institute of Medical Sciences offers experienced faculty, well-equipped laboratories, research facilities, and a structured MBBS curriculum that focuses on strong clinical and diagnostic skills.
