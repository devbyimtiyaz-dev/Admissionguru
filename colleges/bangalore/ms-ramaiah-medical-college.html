About Ramaiah Medical College Admission 2026

M.S. Ramaiah Medical College, commonly known as Ramaiah Medical College, is one of the most reputed private medical colleges in India. Located in Bangalore, Karnataka, the college is known for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and outstanding clinical exposure. It is part of the prestigious Ramaiah Group of Institutions and attracts medical aspirants from across the country.

Admission to Ramaiah Medical College for the MBBS course is conducted strictly through the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). Candidates must qualify NEET and participate in centralized counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) for state quota seats and the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) for All India Quota seats.

One of the major strengths of Ramaiah Medical College is its excellent clinical training. The college is attached to Ramaiah Teaching Hospital, which has a high patient inflow and provides extensive hands-on exposure across multiple medical specialties. This ensures that students develop strong diagnostic and clinical skills during their MBBS training.

The institution offers state-of-the-art laboratories, modern lecture halls, research facilities, and experienced faculty members. The MBBS curriculum is designed to balance theoretical learning with practical application, preparing students for postgraduate studies, competitive exams, and medical practice.

Why Choose Ramaiah Medical College?

Ramaiah Medical College is preferred by MBBS aspirants due to its strong academic reputation, advanced hospital facilities, and consistent results in medical education. The college offers a structured learning environment and excellent exposure to real-world medical practice.

Graduates from Ramaiah Medical College are well-placed in leading hospitals, pursue postgraduate medical education, or establish successful medical careers in India and abroad. With its legacy of excellence and commitment to healthcare education, Ramaiah Medical College remains a top choice for MBBS admission in Karnataka and India.
