About Bangalore Medical College Admission 2026

Bangalore Medical College & Research Institute (BMCRI) is one of the oldest and most prestigious government medical colleges in Karnataka. Located in the heart of Bangalore, the institution is known for its academic excellence, affordable education, and outstanding clinical exposure.

Admission to the MBBS course at Bangalore Medical College is conducted strictly through NEET. Eligible candidates must participate in centralized counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) for state quota seats.

BMCRI is affiliated with major government hospitals such as Victoria Hospital, Vani Vilas Hospital, and Bowring & Lady Curzon Hospital. These hospitals provide massive patient inflow, ensuring extensive hands-on clinical training for MBBS students.

The college offers modern laboratories, experienced faculty, research facilities, and a strong focus on practical medical education. The MBBS program prepares students for postgraduate studies, competitive exams, and government or private medical practice.

Why Choose Bangalore Medical College?

Bangalore Medical College is highly preferred due to its government status, affordable fee structure, excellent clinical exposure, and strong academic legacy. It consistently ranks among the top medical colleges in Karnataka.
