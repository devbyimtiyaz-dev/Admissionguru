About St. John's Medical College Admission 2026

St. John's Medical College, Bangalore is one of the most prestigious medical institutions in India. Known for its academic excellence, ethical medical education, and outstanding clinical exposure, the college attracts top medical aspirants from across the country.

Admission to the MBBS course at St. John's Medical College is strictly based on NEET qualification. Candidates must participate in centralized counseling conducted by the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) for deemed university seats.

The college is attached to St. John's Medical College Hospital, a multi-specialty tertiary care hospital with a high patient inflow. Students receive extensive hands-on training across all major medical departments.

St. John's Medical College emphasizes holistic medical education, combining strong theoretical foundations with practical clinical exposure, research opportunities, and community health programs.
