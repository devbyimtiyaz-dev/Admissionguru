About BMS College of Engineering Admission 2026

BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE) is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering colleges in India. Established in 1946, BMSCE is located in Bangalore, Karnataka, and is known for its strong academic legacy, excellent faculty, and consistent placement performance. The college attracts engineering aspirants from across the country every year.

BMS College of Engineering offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines including Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. Admission to B.Tech programs is conducted through entrance examinations such as KCET for state quota seats, COMEDK for management quota seats, and JEE Main where applicable.

The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). BMSCE is recognized for its rigorous academic curriculum, emphasis on research, and strong industry collaborations.

BMS College of Engineering provides students with modern laboratories, advanced research centers, innovation hubs, and excellent infrastructure. The institution focuses on practical learning, internships, and industry exposure, ensuring students are well-prepared for competitive engineering careers.
